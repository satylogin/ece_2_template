RESEARCH

The Institute aims to inculcate the scientific temper into its students so as to exhort them to follow their dreams, and to contribute to the nation with various new developments. With this in mind we offer our students a chance to pursue their own heartfelt impulse, and choose their projects from among the many availaible fields. Each field offers its own set of experts, who would guide and test the students in their endeavour.
